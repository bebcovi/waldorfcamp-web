Here you will have an opportunity to learn the basics of swimming, windsurfing, kayaking and standup paddleboarding. You will also learn how to tie basic knots needed for sailing, about winds, sea currents and swimming lessons for people of all ages!

## Programme

### Windsurfing

  - how to assemble a windsurfing board
  - getting used to the board
  - basic elements of windsurfing

### Kayaking

  - basics of kayaking
  - organizing a kayak tour
  - environmental actions of removing plastic from unapproachable beaches

### Standup paddleboarding

  - techniques of rowing on the board

As part of the activities above, there will also be activities on the land, such as the following lectures:

  - parts of a sailing boat and a windsurfing board
  - types of knots required for windsurfing
  - meteorology--winds, water currents etc.
